About

What we believe

At Digital Misfits, we believe that misfits are not the exception, but the new norm in a digital working world. People with odd CVs, self-taught talents and unconventional interests. It is the dissenters, the supposed eccentrics and non-conformists who courageously break out of the social corset - in search of a space for self-realization, creating a world that they have always dreamed of. People who are not satisfied with the status quo and, as unrecognized talents, use digitalization to actively shape the future of work and society.

Digital Misfits Festival Recap

For talented and curious people working in the digital space and are interested in changing the world we're living in, the Digital Misfits Festival is the event to attend. Meet like-minded individuals and discuss what it really needs to change the working world for the better. Attend keynotes and workshops from a variety of fields and dive more into topics, that you are really curious about. Or just go with the flow and connect with other people at one of the many special happenings at the Digital Misfits Festival.
